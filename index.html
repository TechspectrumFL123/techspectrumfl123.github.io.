Our Story

During her sophomore year of high school, Avni Thoppae founded TechSpectrum. She
brought together a group of driven students to form the team. They collaborated closely
to build TechSpectrum, driven by a shared passion for technology. Their dedication and
hard work led to the project's success. Today, TechSpectrum stands as a testament to
their innovation and teamwork.

Our Mission

TechSpectrum is dedicated to increasing computer science awareness among high
school students. We offer a wide range of programs designed to inspire and educate the
next generation of tech leaders. Our programs include internships, where students can
apply their comp sci skills professionally, and bootcamps, which provide learning
experiences in various tech disciplines. We also offer research opportunities, allowing
students to engage in research articles and contribute their knowledge of technological
facts to the world. TechSpectrum hosts workshops and mentorship programs, providing
students with numerous ways to develop their skills and network with like-minded peers
and professionals. Our mission is to help students find their passion and build a solid
foundation in computer science.
